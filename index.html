About Us

Flavour Feast was born from a passion for creating beautiful, delicious experiences that bring people together. We believe that great food is more than just sustenance - it is an art form, a conversation starter, and a memory maker.

Terms & Conditions

Please review our service terms before submitting your quote request:

  • Orders must be placed at least Seven (7) days in advance.
  • A 50% deposit is required to secure your order.
    • The deposit is non-refundable after the order is placed.
    • The remaining balance must be paid prior to order delivery.
  • Delivery Fees:
    • North Vancouver & West Vancouver: $40
    • Downtown Vancouver: $50-$60
  • Minimum order amount: $250
  • Orders totaling less than $600 are limited to a maximum of five items.
  • Table setup and delivery fee is 35% of the total order cost (for finger food events).
  • Fresh flower bouquets for event decor are available starting from $85+.
